Privacy Policy

Liquid Glass LiveBook — Last updated April 16, 2026

Overview

Liquid Glass LiveBook is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

Liquid Glass LiveBook does not collect any data from you. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or telemetry are gathered
  • No crash reports are sent (unless you opt in via your device settings)
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • No advertising identifiers are accessed

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Bookmarks — pages you have bookmarked for quick reference
  • Highlights and notes — text you have highlighted and any notes attached
  • Theme preferences — your chosen text and code display themes

This data never leaves your device. It is not synced to any server, cloud service, or third party. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

Internet Access

Liquid Glass LiveBook does not require an internet connection. All content is bundled within the app. The app makes no network requests.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data processing services.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
